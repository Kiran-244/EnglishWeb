Gendered Identities and the Disciplining of Women

A Critical Reading of A Life Less Ordinary by Baby Halder

This research project examines the intersections of gender, identity, and social conditioning through a critical engagement with Baby Halder’s autobiographical work A Life Less Ordinary. The study situates the text within contemporary feminist discourse and lived narratives of marginalization.
