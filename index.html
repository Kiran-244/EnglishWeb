About Me

Ms. Binderpal Kaur is an Assistant Professor of English Literature at Akal University, Talwandi Sabo. Her academic interests include British Literature and Commonwealth Studies. She is actively engaged in teaching, academic research, and literary scholarship, with a strong commitment to higher education.
